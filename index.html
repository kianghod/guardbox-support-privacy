GuardBox Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

GuardBox is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide our secure note-taking service:

1.1 User Content

  • Notes and Stories: All your notes, stories, and content are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
  • Images: Photos you add to your notes are stored locally on your device
  • Settings: App preferences like theme, font size, and passcode are stored locally

1.2 Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used only when you choose to add photos to your notes
  • Photo Library: Used only when you choose to select existing photos for your notes
  • Microphone: Used only for text-to-speech functionality when you activate it
Important: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our servers. All your content remains on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal data, we don't use it for any purpose. Your data is used only locally on your device for:

  • Displaying your notes and stories
  • Applying your chosen settings and preferences
  • Providing text-to-speech functionality
  • Managing your passcode and security settings

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any of your data with third parties. Since all your data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to it and cannot share it.

4. Data Security

Your data security is our top priority:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • Passcode protection ensures only you can access your notes
  • Decoy mode provides additional security layers
  • No data is transmitted over the internet

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data persists until you delete it through the app
  • You can delete individual stories or all stories at any time
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all local data
  • We have no control over or access to your data

6. Third-Party Services

GuardBox uses the following third-party services for core functionality:

  • Flutter TTS: For text-to-speech functionality (data processed locally)
  • Image Picker: For camera and photo library access (data processed locally)
  • SharedPreferences: For local data storage (data stored locally only)

None of these services transmit your data to external servers.

7. Children's Privacy

GuardBox is not specifically designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@guardbox.app
  • Through the app: Settings > About Us > Contact Support

10. Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Modify or delete any content at any time
  • Export your data by copying from the app
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
Summary: GuardBox is a privacy-first app that keeps all your data on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal information. Your notes are yours alone.
